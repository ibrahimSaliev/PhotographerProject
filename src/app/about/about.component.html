A little bit about me

I am almost always overwhelmed at how beautiful and simple life can be simultaneously. I had a nomadic childhood and met so many people along the way. I believe this gave me an incredible ability to meet them. I absolutely wear my heart on my sleeve and am forever falling in love with the people I meet. I chat to everyone, I love to talk and to find out as much as I can about each person. I find people to be fascinating.

The things that light my SOUL on fire - traveling to new places, the view at the end of a hike, road trips without a plan, making authentic connections.
